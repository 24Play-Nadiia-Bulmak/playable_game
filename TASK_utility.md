Improve the structure, consistency, and maintainability of your codebase.
Create simple utilities to reduce duplication and simplify future development.
### 1. **Refactor Your Code**

- Organize files and folders clearly.
- Clean up duplicate logic or repeated hard-coded values.
- Extract utility functions or services where needed.
- Ensure naming is consistent and meaningful.

### 2. **Improve Reusability**

- Turn any repeated logic into reusable components or helper functions.
- Make sure configuration is exposed via variables/constants instead of hardcoded inside logic.
- Group similar logic (e.g., input, timers, VFX) for easier debugging and future scaling.
